NTR

The first-look poster has undoubtedly lived up to the expectations.

NTR is currently working with Koratala Siva on an action entertainer. This marks the 30th film for the actor.

The first look poster showcases NTR in a stunning avatar holding a dagger. His intense expression and unparalleled swag are sure to give goosebumps to the masses.

Bollywood sizzling diva Janhvi Kapoor plays the lead actress, while Saif Ali Khan plays the nemesis of NTR.

The film has its music composed by Anirudh Ravichander. Devara will hit the screens on 5th April 2024.
